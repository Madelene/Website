Let me introduce myself....

I have been a professional freelance musician (flute and piano) for over a decade. I have played flute for a national Windex ad, played with Yo-Yo Ma, Jewel, and Johnny Mathis, and have performed all over the world, including the Lyric Opera of Chicago, Milwaukee Symphony, Hawaii Opera, Hawaii Symphony, and the Beijing Philharmonic in Beijing, China. Despite having a passion to perform and teach, the lack of security in this field pushed me to move my life a different direction...and possibly a better one, ultimately.

After doing a bit of research (by Googling, ironically enough) I discovered Wyncode.

As part of the week 3 curriculum at Wyncode Academy, I am putting up this website using HTML and CSS. The first two weeks focused on learning and completing projects using Ruby. Please feel free to check out my Github to view the various projects we have completed. After Ruby, we covered Internet protocols and had two days of lectures on HTML and CSS. This website is going up over a weekend to get practical experience using these technologies and to have a site to show off what we've learned. I'll be adding to it as the weeks progress and as we learn more relevant tools, like JavaScript.
